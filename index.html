About This Game

🏴‍☠️ You are sent on a mission to the middle of the ocean.
🏴‍☠️ You must uncover the mystery of the Bermuda Triangle...
✅ Survive 100 Days and get rich!

Open-World Survival game where exploration and base building are the keys to survival.

HOW TO PLAY:

🪝 Grab resources or hook them with your Harpoon.
🔨 Build a raft and structures for your base.
🗺️ Explore the sea and visit mysterious islands.
⚔️ Find weapons and prepare your defenses!

All Item & Boss List - 100 Days at Sea Roblox

Here is a comprehensive list of items and bosses in the game "100 Days at Sea" on Roblox:

    Items

  • Wood Plank: Used for building structures and crafting items.

  • Small/Big Gasoline: Used to increase more Campfires or if shot it can cause an explosion.

  • Crafting Table: A crafting station where you can create various items.

  • Food: Essential for survival, restores hunger.

  • Crab Trap: Used to catch food automatically over time.

  • Fishing Rod: Used to catch fish. You can also give fish to the Fisherman to receive items.

  • Sawmill: Processes wood into planks over time ( 3 wood per planks ).

  • Oil Drill: Used to extract oil from the ground over time. ( requires 3 wood for operation ).

  • Ammo Create: Used to craft ammunition for weapons overtime ( requires 3 iron for operation ).

    Weapons

  • Hatchet: A versatile tool used for chopping wood and as a weapon.
  • Grenade: A throwable explosive device used for combat.
  • Buku Boku Spear: A ranged weapon that can be used to attack enemies from a distance. (This item can be dropped if you killed a buku boku)
  • Harpoon: Used to climb and can also be used as a weapon.
  • Flintlock: A type of firearm used for hunting and combat.
  • Blunderbuss: A short-range firearm that fires multiple projectiles at once.
  • Rifle: A long-range firearm that is more accurate and more powerful
  • Star Magma: A long-range wizard staff can drop fireballs/meteors from the sky. (This item cannot be crafted unless you defeat the boss in the volcano.)

    Boss

  • The Vessel Pirate: Pirate vessels can be defeated by using ranged weapons to avoid their numerous attacks or by crafting a Kraken Tail using 30 wood, 30 iron, and 12 ghost jars at a crafting table. After defeating a pirate vessel, all its corpses can be turned into ghost jars at a crafting table.

  • The Kraken: Kraken will appear if there is a notification in Roblox and will attack your island, you can defeat it with melee and ranged weapons, if you defeat the kraken, it will drop a Ghost Jar.

  • The Golem: You can find it in some volcanoes by climbing using Harpoon, then defeating 3 fiery golems in different places (usually in the corner of the volcano on the MAP), then bring the three fiery golems into the volcano in the middle then drop the three golems into the middle and will fight the Final Boss Golem, if you succeed in defeating you will get STAR MAGMA.
